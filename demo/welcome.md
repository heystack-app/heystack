# Welcome to the heystack demo

This is a small, neutral knowledge base about coffee, in several languages and
formats, so you can try heystack right away. Everything here is example content.

## Things to try

- "How do I make a pour over?"
- "What is the ideal coffee to water ratio?"
- "Where does coffee come from?" (the answer lives in a Spanish document)
- "Quel est le ratio pour un espresso ?" (ask in French)
- "Wie viel Koffein hat eine Tasse Kaffee?" (ask in German)

Ask in your own language. heystack searches across all of these by meaning, not
just keywords, so an English question can find a Spanish or German document.

Every answer cites its sources. Click a citation to open the original document.
